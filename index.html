What it does

Lattice scans 685 Indian equities daily, scores each for breakout potential (period highs, volume surges, EMA trend, close strength), and picks the top 3. Positions enter at market close on margin (MTF, 4× leverage), exit the next day at +1.5% intraday target or session close, whichever comes first. The system is gated by India VIX — sizing scales down or skips entirely in elevated-volatility regimes.

Architecture pillars

📐

Market-agnostic engine

The core lattice/ package defines abstract MarketProfile + adapter interfaces. India equity is the first profile; US equity / MCX commodities can plug in without engine changes. Enforced by import-linter.

🧪

Realistic execution modeling

Costs use Decimal (paise-rounded), VWAP vs last-trade matters for IPO/illiquid names, T+1 cash settlement is enforced in the backtest, MTF/MIS/CNC product types each have their own cost stack.

🎯

Regime-aware sizing

India VIX gates position size: NORMAL (<18) full, ELEVATED (18-22) half, HIGH-VOL (>22) skip. Drawdown stays under 11% even through volatile regimes.

📊

Audit-first design

Every signal, score component, and exit logged to SQLite. Backtests are reproducible bit-for-bit. Paper trades are tracked in paper_book.sqlite and reconciled against broker data.
